Breathing Exercises

Morning Routine

Wim Hof Method

1s in • 1s out
10 minutes

Rapid breathing technique that increases oxygen levels and activates the sympathetic nervous system.

Box Breathing

6s in • 6s hold • 6s out • 6s hold
6 minutes

Four-square breathing pattern that promotes focus and reduces stress through balanced timing.

Coherent Breathing

5s in • 5s out
4 minutes

Smooth, rhythmic breathing that optimizes heart rate variability and promotes relaxation.

Midday Routine

Nadi Shodhana

6s in • 6s hold • 6s out • 6s hold
5 minutes

Alternate nostril breathing that balances the nervous system and calms the mind.

Evening Routine

Extended Exhale

4s in • 16s out
8 minutes

Long exhales activate the parasympathetic nervous system for deep relaxation.

Bhramari Pranayama

4s in • 8s hum
4 minutes

Humming breath that creates calming vibrations and reduces mental chatter.
